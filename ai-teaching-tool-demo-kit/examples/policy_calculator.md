# Policy Calculator

## Teaching Goal

Help students see how policy parameters affect outcomes such as tax burden, benefit eligibility, market equilibrium, or budget tradeoffs.

## Classroom Flow

The instructor chooses a policy setting, students suggest parameter changes, and the tool displays simulated consequences.

## Student Actions

Students enter proposed policy values, vote on options, or compare scenarios.

## Instructor Actions

Set baseline assumptions, approve scenarios, reveal output charts, and export scenario records.

## Data Saved

Timestamp, session ID, participant or group ID, parameter values, calculated outcomes, and notes.

## Possible Scoring Or Payoff Logic

Scoring may be unnecessary. If used, compare student predictions to calculated outcomes or reward meeting policy targets.

## What To Test

Test boundary values, invalid inputs, formula transparency, export fields, and whether assumptions are visible.

## Possible Extensions

Add multiple policy targets, saved scenarios, class votes, sensitivity analysis, or downloadable charts.
