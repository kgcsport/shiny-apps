# Prediction Market

## Teaching Goal

Help students practice probabilistic forecasting, information aggregation, or belief updating.

## Classroom Flow

The instructor posts a question, students submit predictions, the class discusses, and the instructor reveals the outcome later.

## Student Actions

Students submit a probability, prediction, confidence score, or small simulated trade.

## Instructor Actions

Create questions, open and close prediction windows, reveal outcomes, score predictions, and export data.

## Data Saved

Timestamp, session ID, question ID, participant ID, prediction, confidence, outcome, and score.

## Possible Scoring Or Payoff Logic

Use a simple absolute-error score, Brier score, or simulated payoff based on prediction accuracy.

## What To Test

Test duplicate submissions, late submissions, outcome entry, scoring, anonymity, and summary displays.

## Possible Extensions

Add team predictions, multiple questions, calibration charts, or before-and-after belief updates.
